Impact of AI on Business Performance

Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) adoption on business performance within the IT sector.
The analysis is based on survey data collected from 179 professionals working in IT organizations.

The goal of the project is to explore how different levels of AI adoption influence key business performance indicators such as productivity, operational efficiency, revenue growth, and employee satisfaction.

This project demonstrates SQL-based data analysis and business insight generation from survey data.


Business Questions

The analysis focuses on answering the following questions:
	1.	How widely is AI adopted among IT companies?
	2.	Which company sizes adopt AI more frequently?
	3.	Does AI adoption improve productivity?
	4.	Does AI adoption contribute to revenue growth?
	5.	Does AI usage influence employee satisfaction?
	6.	What is the relationship between AI adoption and overall business performance?


Dataset

The dataset contains 179 survey responses from professionals working in the IT sector.

Dataset Features
	•	respondent_id – Unique identifier for each survey participant
	•	company_size – Size of the organization (Small, Medium, Large)
	•	role – Job role of the respondent
	•	ai_adoption_level – Level of AI adoption in the organization
	•	productivity_improvement – Percentage improvement in productivity
	•	decision_speed – Improvement in decision-making speed
	•	operational_efficiency – Improvement in operational efficiency
	•	revenue_growth – Percentage increase in revenue
	•	employee_satisfaction – Employee satisfaction score (1–5)

SQL Analysis

The analysis was performed using SQL queries to explore patterns and relationships within the dataset.

Key analyses include:
	•	AI adoption distribution
	•	AI adoption by company size
	•	Productivity improvement by AI adoption level
	•	Revenue growth by AI adoption level
	•	Employee satisfaction by AI adoption level
	•	Comparison of multiple business performance indicators across AI adoption levels


Key Insights

Key insights from the analysis include:
	•	Most companies are in the early stages of AI adoption, with low and moderate adoption levels being the most common.
	•	Companies with higher AI adoption tend to report greater productivity improvements.
	•	Organizations using AI show stronger revenue growth on average.
	•	AI adoption contributes to better operational efficiency and faster decision-making.
	•	Employees in companies using AI report slightly higher satisfaction levels.

Detailed insights can be found in the insights.md file.
